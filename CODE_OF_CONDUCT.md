# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our standards

Positive behavior includes empathy, respect, constructive feedback, responsibility, and a focus on what is best for the community. Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discriminatory language, threats, sexualized attention, publishing another person's private information, or other conduct reasonably considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct. Report conduct concerns privately to the project maintainer through the contact method listed on the maintainer's GitHub profile.
